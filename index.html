Travel tips

  • Plan smart: Book flights and stays at least 3–4 weeks in advance for better prices.
  • Pack light: Check weather and carry essentials (ID, meds, charger, light jacket).
  • Stay safe: Keep copies of documents and use official transport providers.
  • Local culture: Respect customs; try local food and support small businesses.
  • Budgeting: Track daily spend; set aside cash for souvenirs and emergencies.
